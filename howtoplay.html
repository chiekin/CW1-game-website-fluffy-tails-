how to play

     you are a fearless girl navigating a wild world to search for carrots to give to your bunnies while battling against a sneaky mutant lizard!

survive by dodging its attacks, ARROW KEYS to move around, fight back with your slashes, and keep your wits about you. == PRESS 'E' to slash!

gather the magical carrots that will pop up to boost your score, but watch out -- those tricky lizards get faster the more you score!

can you outsmart them and keep your hearts intact? good luck!
